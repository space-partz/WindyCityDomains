What is Shared Hosting?

Shared web hosting is the most affordable type of domain hosting available. You share a server with various companies, each with its own specified amount of storage space defined by the hosting package. This setup allows for cost-effective hosting solutions, accommodating possibly thousands of websites on the same server.

What are the Benefits of Shared Hosting?

  • Cost Effectiveness - It's the most affordable option, suitable for smaller, newer sites with sufficient bandwidth, power, and memory.
  • Efficiency - Pay only for what you need, with managed resources to optimize your expenditure.
  • Convenience - Managed by skilled experts, focusing on server health to let you focus on your business.
  • Customization - Your website remains fully customizable, despite sharing server space.
